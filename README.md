# Population And Recruitment
Mod Link Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/mountandblade2bannerlord/mods/8840

- Adds recruitment options to castles
- Noble recruits can only be spawned in castles
- Normal troops can only be spawned in castles and towns
- Villages only spawn militia now
- The population of a village is determined by it's militia count
- Spawn quality is based on village hearth/Settlement prosperity

Once a unit spawns it subtracts a militia from the settlement/village it spawns in. Villages now only spawn militia and veteran militia. Towns spawn normal militia and Normal troops, Castles spawn Normal militia, Normal Troops, and Elite units.


The Default Spawn quality for castles is Militia, Normal Troops, Elite Troops where prosperity determines the spawn chance of each.

Raiding villages is now more impactful as it reduces the supply of troops a Kingdom can recruit from.

Fiefs can only be recruited from by the owner clan. This stops Ai clans from stealing all your higher quality troops and makes owning fiefs more important.

Since Elite troops can only spawn in castles, it allows castles to be more important to a kingdom as it's the only supply of high quality troops.

Ai has been modified to visit their clan owned Castles/Towns to allow recruitment (Thanks to Sh1ny4 for allowing me to modify his logic for it).

Prosperity reduction from troop spawn can be set to any number (defaulted to zero)

Village militia growth rate has been adjusted for balancing purposes (Configurable)

Mercenary spawn rate has been adjusted (Configurable). They are now more important to hire as they're the only source of upgradable troops lords can hire if their clan doesn't own a fief

Highly configurable MCM, allowing you to adjust values as you desire. Especially for mods that increase militia/prosperity count.

Formulas for Troop Quality Spawn change is in the images section
